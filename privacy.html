Privacy Policy

How Postlewhite Plumbing Installations handles your personal information. Last updated 10 May 2026.

Who we are

This site is operated by Postlewhite Plumbing Installations, a Gas Safe registered plumbing & heating business based in Macclesfield, Cheshire, United Kingdom.

For any privacy queries, contact us by email at possplumboffice@gmail.com or by phone on 07902 025580.

What information we collect

We collect two types of information:

Information you give us when you contact us by phone, text, WhatsApp, email or Facebook Messenger: your name, phone number, email address, the address of the property where work is needed, and the details of the plumbing job.

Information collected automatically when you visit this website, via Google Analytics: pages visited, time on site, approximate location (city / region from IP address), device and browser type, and referring website. Your IP address is anonymised by Google before it's stored. This is only collected if you accept analytics cookies.

How we use your information

We use the information you provide to respond to your enquiry, prepare quotes, carry out the plumbing or heating work you've asked us to do, send invoices, and follow up on jobs.

We use website analytics to understand which pages are useful, where visitors come from, and to improve the site over time. Analytics data is aggregated and not used to identify individual visitors.

Lawful basis

Under UK GDPR, we rely on the following legal bases for processing your information:

  • Performance of a contract when you've asked us to carry out work for you.
  • Legitimate interests for responding to enquiries and basic record-keeping (in a way you'd reasonably expect from a small business).
  • Consent for analytics cookies, set only after you click Accept on the cookie banner. You can withdraw consent at any time using the Cookie settings link in the footer.
  • Legal obligation for record-keeping requirements such as tax records.

Cookies

This site uses one category of cookies:

Google Analytics (GA4), sets cookies named _ga and _ga_QJ4YRCMSEJ that help us count and understand visitors. These cookies are only set if you click Accept on the cookie banner. By default they're disabled (we use Google Consent Mode v2). They expire after up to 13 months.

You can change your choice at any time by clicking in the footer of any page.

Who we share information with

Google (Google Ireland Limited), for website analytics, only when you've accepted analytics cookies. Google may transfer data to the United States; transfers are protected under the EU-US Data Privacy Framework.

Meta Platforms Ireland Ltd, if you choose to message us via Facebook Messenger or WhatsApp, those messages are processed by Meta on their platforms. We're not responsible for Meta's separate privacy practices; see Meta's Privacy Policy.

HMRC and our accountant, for tax and bookkeeping purposes where legally required.

We do not sell your personal information, and we do not share it for advertising purposes.

How long we keep your information

  • Enquiry messages (email / WhatsApp / Messenger): up to 12 months unless we have an ongoing business relationship.
  • Job records and invoices: 6 years, to comply with HMRC tax requirements.
  • Analytics data: up to 14 months in Google Analytics.

Your rights

Under UK GDPR you have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Correct anything that's wrong
  • Have your information deleted (where we don't have a legal need to keep it)
  • Restrict or object to certain processing
  • Withdraw consent at any time (for analytics, via the Cookie settings link)
  • Receive a copy of your data in a portable format
  • Complain to the Information Commissioner's Office at ico.org.uk if you're unhappy with how we've handled your data

To exercise any of these rights, email us at possplumboffice@gmail.com.

Changes to this policy

If we make significant changes to how we handle your data, we'll update this page and refresh the "last updated" date at the top.
