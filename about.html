About Us

Local plumbing, done the right way

Postlewhite Plumbing Installations is a Macclesfield-based plumbing & heating business with over 17 years' experience in the trade.

Trustworthy, honest, reliable

Postlewhite Plumbing was built on doing things the right way. After 17+ years in the trade, covering everything from emergency leaks to complete bathroom installations, we've found that customers want three simple things from a plumber: honesty about the problem, a fair price, and quality work that lasts.

That's what we deliver, every time. It's why most of our work comes from neighbours, recommendations and repeat customers, and why every reviewer on our Facebook page recommends us.

Gas Safe Registered

All gas work in the UK must be carried out by a Gas Safe registered engineer, and we are. Whether it's a boiler service, a new gas hob or a full boiler replacement, you can be confident the work is being done safely and to current regulations.

Postlewhite Plumbing Installations is listed on the official Gas Safe Register under registration number 622530, search by name, registration number or our phone number to verify directly.

What we're qualified to work on

Our Gas Safe registration covers (among other things):

  • Gas boilers & central heating systems
  • Domestic cookers, hobs and range cookers
  • Gas water heaters
  • Vented hot water storage cylinders
  • Gas installation pipework
  • Combustion performance gas analysis
  • Heating system controls, flues and extensions (Building Regulations work)
